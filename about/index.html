Saffrony Institute of Technology
Fusion is the once-a-year cultural fest of Saffrony Institute Of Technology. It’s one of the most spectacular fests over colleges of Gujarat. This carnival irradiates the essence and ethos of saffronites.
Foremost ,stage events of our festival are enkindled by famed people. Additionally, the assorted number of cultural events of belles-lettres, melody & theatricals embellishes the glowing glamour of the fest .

Fusion

Fusion remains an unrivaled platform, one and all showcased their brilliance and relished the time of their online college life. A spectacle of music, dance and arts, fusion endures and always has been a culture you can ne’er get enough of. Apart from lavish shows like star night with famous artists, the various competitive events were conducted and heavily rewarding cash prizes. Fusion continues its legacy, getting gigantic, finer and stronger with each passing year.

OUR TEAM

Fusion is the amalgamation of cultural extravaganza and enthused spirits. Each year, we aim to outdo ourselves and raise the bar even higher. This is possible only through the dedication and sincere efforts of Team Fusion- a group of like minded and passionate individuals who work together to make each iteration a phenomena in its own.

Our Legacy

Being one of the largest cultural fest among North Indian colleges, Fusion boasts of having hosted major celebrities and performance from top notch artists, engaging competitions and footfall of thousands. The Fusion main stage has been graced by artists including Neeti Mohan, Farhan Akhtar, Benny Dayal, comedians such as Anubhav SIngh Bassi, Aakash Gupta and many more.
